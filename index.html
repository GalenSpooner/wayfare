THE MECHANISM

Empty seats are worth something — to you.

A shared van running to the airport has near-zero extra cost to carry one more rider. Operators would rather fill that seat at a discount than run it empty.

We negotiate that discount directly with each operator and pass it straight to you. Same wholesale-rate logic hotels and tour companies have used for decades — nobody had brought it to the shuttle van down the street until now.
